Who We Are

Moksh Delights is a home-grown food brand born from a simple passion: to bring authentic, homemade Indian flavors to every home. Though we are new in the online market, our products have already earned the trust and love of customers through years of offline sales and positive word-of-mouth.

Our founder, Devendra Yadav, started this journey with a vision to preserve traditional Indian cooking methods while meeting modern food safety and quality standards. Every pickle we prepare and every spice we grind carries the essence of authentic Indian culinary heritage.
